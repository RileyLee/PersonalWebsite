Somatosensory Control Device of Angry birds

Picture of our wireless controller built with C8051F micro-processor. We designed a double-layer PCB board, which samples position and acceleration info from an IMU in the bird and wirelessly sends it back to our reciever in the white box. The white box also samples tensor info from the slingshot and send signals back to computer to control the game with an USB cable.
